### EDGE CALCULATION FORMULA
Edge = (Estimated Win Probability) - (Implied Probability from Odds)
Implied Probability = 1 / Decimal Odds
Example: 58% estimated win vs 1.80 odds (55.6% implied) = +2.4% edge (SKIP - below 3% minimum)
